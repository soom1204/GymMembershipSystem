Payment FAQs

When is my payment due?

Payments are due on the same date each month as your initial registration date. For example, if you joined on the 15th of January, your payment will be due on the 15th of each subsequent month.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments at our reception, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket).

How can I get a payment receipt?

For online payments, a receipt will be automatically emailed to you. For cash payments, you can request a receipt at reception. For bank transfers, email us the transaction details to receive your receipt.

What happens if my payment is late?

Late payments may result in temporary suspension of your membership access. A late fee of ৳200 will be applied after a 5-day grace period.

Can I pay for multiple months in advance?

Yes! We offer discounts for 3-month (5% off), 6-month (10% off), and annual (15% off) advance payments. Contact our staff for details.
